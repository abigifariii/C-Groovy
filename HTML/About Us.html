About us

C'Groovy is a leading music streaming platform established in 2016, committed to bringing a world of music to everyone’s fingertips. With innovation, passion, and community at its core, C’Groovy continues to redefine the music experience for millions of users globally.

Our Journey

2016 - The Beginning

C'Groovy was founded with a simple yet powerful dream to make music discovery effortless and accessible for everyone. What started as a small project by a group of music enthusiasts quickly grew into a bold vision for the future of streaming.

2017 - The First Beat

After months of hard work and innovation, C'Groovy launched its first version, offering over 100,000 songs across multiple genres. This early success ignited a passionate community of listeners who believed in the power of music to connect people.

2018 - Personalization

To create a truly unique experience for every user, we introduced a personalized recommendation engine powered by cutting-edge AI technology. Listeners could now discover songs that felt tailor-made for them, making every moment with C'Groovy.

2020 - Growing Without Limits

Our music library expanded rapidly, reaching over 10 million tracks from artists around the globe. We forged new partnerships with record labels and independent creators, ensuring that C'Groovy had something for every taste, mood, and moment.

2022 - Seamless Listening

Recognizing the need for a smoother experience, we launched seamless cross-device syncing. Whether users were switching from a morning jog with their phone to a relaxing evening with their laptop, C'Groovy kept the music flowing effortlessly.

2025 - Vibrant Community

Today, C'Groovy is more than just a streaming service. We are a global community of music lovers, artists, and innovators, constantly pushing the boundaries of what music can mean. With every beat, every playlist, and every shared moment.
